Episode WDD 330


In the difficult times where the evil Empire dominates all galaxies, a galactic epidemic called KOVID – XIX spread, killing thousands of people. The allies in an unusual effort, using all possible medical resources, made a vaccine that controlled the situation.

The allies passed a task called challenge 02 where each member had to create a system using the resources learned in the class during the semester. All members have committed to overcome this challenge.

With the help of Master Jedi Andrew, commander of quadrant WDD 330, of the great galaxy BYU-I, and the members of team 01, composed of padawans Ana, of the MEX galaxy, Emiles, of the USA galaxy, José, of the CHI galaxy, Luis, of the unknown galaxy and Hudson, from galaxy BRA, during 14 time streams, the team won several battles with only one casualty of padawan Ana. This loss shook the team up a bit, but with the help of the Force, the team continued on their journey.

After the 14 time streams, the team managed to finish its mission and may have a little time flow to get ready for new missions that will appear next.
